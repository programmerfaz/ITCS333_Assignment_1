Arts and Craft

Arts and crafts in kindergarten help kids improve their hand skills and creativity. They enjoy making things like paintings and crafts while learning to share and work together.

Music and Dance

Music and dance in kindergarten help kids develop rhythm and coordination. They enjoy singing songs, moving to music, and expressing themselves through dance. It’s a fun way to build confidence, teamwork, and creativity!

Outdoor Activities

Outdoor activities in kindergarten help kids stay active and healthy. They enjoy running, playing games, and exploring nature, which helps improve their physical skills and teamwork. It's a fun way to learn, grow, and stay energized!

Story time

Story time in kindergarten helps kids develop language and listening skills. They enjoy hearing exciting tales and using their imagination to picture the stories. It’s a fun way to spark creativity and a love for reading!

Resources

At Sunshine Kindergarten, we keep parents involved in their child's learning journey with daily updates on classroom activities and monthly newsletters featuring upcoming events and learning goals.

Parents can attend regular meetings to discuss their child's progress and view photos and videos of class activities. We also provide learning resources for home, like worksheets and craft ideas, and share health and safety guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all.
